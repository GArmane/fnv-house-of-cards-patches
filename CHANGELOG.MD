House of Cards - Misc. Changes

- Damage Resistance cap set to 90%. Similar to darthbdaman's Threshold.
- Minimal damage multiplier set to 5%. Similar to darthbdaman's Threshold.
- Cases and hulls has their weights set to realistic values (WIP).
- Change bent tin can recipe from Agua Fria to a boring name.
- Change default timescale to 12. A game day takes 2 hours in real time.
- Hide cure disease boxes from Simple Diseases mod.
- Remove Eyepatch hair option from CKR.
- Change Casino maximum payouts from Simple Economy eXpansion to the following values:
  - Vikki and Vance: 2000 -> 2000
  - Atomic Wrangler: 2000 -> 4000
  - Gomorrah: 5000 -> 6000
  - The Tops: 5000 -> 8000
  - Ultra-Luxe: 5000 -> 10000
  - Sierra Madre: 5000 -> 10000
  - The rationale is to implement some sort of progression in the payouts as you explore more and more casinos in the game. The Atomic Wrangler is sort of a method of gaining enough cash to enter The Strip if you so choose, matching exactly the amount needed to pass through the Gatekeeper - but not enough to get a passport. Also, making the payouts of the top casino in the region being just slightly above the credit check is sort of non-sensical.
- Integration of Simple Economy eXpansion with "P6 - Chinese Assault Rifles" and "Gun and Friends"
